Check out IconFactory.com

By Dori Smith | August 3rd, 2006 | Filed in General, Validation, Web Standards (general)

Skip to comment form

Over the last week we’ve been noticing the short teaser movies at Iconfactory.com. We could tell that something was up, but we weren’t sure quite what. Now, it’s official: they’ve redesigned, and it not only looks great, it’s also standards-compliant XHTML and CSS.

To the folks at IconFactory: great job, and congratulations on your tenth anniversary! And to everyone else: if you didn’t see the movies, or missed one of the six, they’re available here.
